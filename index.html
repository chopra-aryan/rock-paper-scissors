Welcome to the Game Zone

This is the classic Rock, paper, or Scissors. You can make your choice by using the buttons below. Rules are fairly simple, you will make one choice and computer will make one at the same time and then a winner will be declared. There is a good chance that game could draw, Each game has 3 rounds.

Regulations

  • Rock vs Paper = Paper wins!
  • Rock vs Scissors = Rock wins!
  • Paper vs Scissors = Scissors wins!
